    Early Years and Musical Beginnings

    Hailing from East Palo Alto, California, Sean T, originally known as Sean Miguel Thompson, embarked on his musical journey at an early age. His skills were honed, and his industry experience grew as he became a part of the group called Murder One Gangstas.

    Debut Album Triumphs: “Straight from the Streets”

    In 1993, Sean T made waves in the music scene with the release of his debut album, “Straight from the Streets.” The album received acclaim from audiences, serving as Sean T’s breakthrough in the industry. Building on this success, he further solidified his position with the 1996 release of his second album, “Pimp Lyrics & Dollar Signs.”
