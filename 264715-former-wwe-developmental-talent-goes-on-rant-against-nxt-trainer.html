    Former WWE developmental talent Kevin Matthews has been going on a rant against the current head of NXT developmental in Florida, Bill DeMott. It all started with the death of pro wrestler Buddy Roberts, as Matthews tweeted "R.I.P Buddy Roberts. It should of been @BillDeMott and Not you. You actually meant something to the business."

    He then went on a very long Twitter rampage, pretending to be Bill DeMott:

    I don't know how to do a lockup or teach one, but I do know how to get people fired. #DeMottcracy

    I injured 24 FCW students and WWE is blaming me! Its the kids fault for not quitting when they announced I was the new trainer! #DeMottcracy

    — Kevin Matthews (@2CockyKM) November 30, 2012

    John Cenas trainer Rob spoke out against me 3weeks ago on how I was training the kids too hard. I got him fired 24hrs later. #DeMottcracy
