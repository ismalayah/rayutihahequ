    In other Mzansi television news, We reported that Masasa Mbangeni stars in a new drama about human trafficking. One of Mzansi's favourite actresses Masasa Mbangeni is starring in a new South African show. The television show is airing on Mzansi Magic on Mondays and is based on the issue of human trafficking in the country.

    The plot of the show is fitting for a country like Mzansi, where human trafficking has been a cause for concern over the last few years.

    The first episode of the show aired on 4 January, 2021 and it is titled Mzali Wam. Popular Mzansi actor Dumisani Mbebe is also starring in the production as a businessman named Terror.
