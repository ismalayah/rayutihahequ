    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    The Relic Band (Japanese: こだいのうでわ Ancient Bracelet) is a valuable item introduced in Generation V.

    In the core series games

    Price

    In the Generation V games, the Relic Band can be sold to the billionaire maniac in Undella Town for $100000.

    Effect

    The Relic Band serves no practical purpose other than to be sold.
